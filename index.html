About Langhe Bike Tour

Explore the Rolling Hills of Langhe on Two Wheels

Welcome to Langhe Bike Tours , your gateway to an immersive journey through one of Italy’s most picturesque regions. Whether you’re a seasoned cyclist or a casual rider, our tours offer a unique blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation.
Our tours are designed to cater to all levels of fitness and expertise. With both e-bikes and traditional bikes available, you can choose the pace and style that suits you best. Join us to discover the hidden gems of Langhe, from its world-renowned wineries and gastronomic delights to its historic castles and quaint villages.

Electric Bikes

Glide effortlessly through the vineyard-clad landscapes with our state-of-the-art e-bikes. They provide the extra boost you need to conquer the hills and savor the breathtaking views without breaking a sweat.

Mountain Bikes

For the brave at heart who cherish the classic cycling experience, our traditional mountain bikes are the ideal choice. Feel the rush of adrenaline as you navigate through the vineyards and mud, embracing the raw beauty of Langhe’s countryside.
